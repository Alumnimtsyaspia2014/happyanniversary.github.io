When Muniba Met Haseeb
That day changed everything

Our First Meet
3rd March 2010

It was a cheerful sunny morning as I walked into my new school. The people were new, I was feeling nervous as I met my new class fellows for the first time. Then I saw her, she was wearing black squared glasses with short hair and pleasant smile on her face. Her friendly personality blew me away. I had no idea then that this person would become one of the most important person in my life.

Our First Date
Summer of June, 2010

It was a sweet Uth festival invitation by her. I had to go through some trouble to get the passes from her. The festival was mainly about football matches and a concert afterwards. I met her for like 8-10 mins and the butterflies were so strong that I couldn't even talk properly. Weird though, I still remember her saying politely, "Sharma kyun rahe ho!?"

How I Proposed
6th June, 2010

It was summer vacations. I wanted to let her know about my feelings but was nervous about her response. Took the plunge one day and told her about my feelings. Fingers crossed, I sat on my bed waiting for her reply to come back on the facebook chat. When her reply came, I gave a sigh of joy. To me, it was never about how I proposed, it was about how I felt about her.

Since Then, We Are Together
To Present

Thats right! We have been together for 7 years (8th starting). Love is not just about cute texts and hearts, its also about respect, patience and support. If you can put in these, you can achieve happiness out of nothingness.
